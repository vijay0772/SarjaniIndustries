Who We Are

Sarjani Industries is considered as one of the upcoming name in the field of Electrical & Power equipments. We are engaged in Manufacturing of Distribution & Power Transformers, Servo Voltage Stabilizers, HT Two in One Systems, HT Servo Voltage Stabilizers.

The Transformers & voltage controllers are of superior quality and possess more life span. Our commitment is to serve our clients with best quality electrical equipments. We are leading manufacturer of transformers and stabilizers. Our product's Quality and Reliability is the driving forces behind our commitment to total customer satisfaction.

We are also supplier of Transformer laminations, Toroidal cores and EI lamination. it is used for alternative applications like Welding Machines, Voltage Stabilizers. All kind of power transformers and voltage controllers are passed through various quality test stages to produce state of the art technology products. We use high quality Japanese measuring instruments to maintain the dimensional accuracy at highest level. We can give special rates for long term or high quantity value contracts. Our equipments are designed for producing high efficiency at lower costs with lesser Payback Period for the customers.
