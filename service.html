Lamination Cutting

Transformer core lamination for Power & Distribution transformers is manufactured from various grades of Cold Rolled Grain Oriented ( CRGO ) steel.

Lamination in various shapes & sizes are slit and cut to required accurate geometrical shapes with 90° & 45° cutting systems. The manufacturing of Cold Rolled Grain Oriented CRGO Quality Lamination involves a high degree precision engineering.

Sliting Service

Slitting is the first and foremost manufacturing process in order to obtain any type of cut lamination. Indeed, it is the heart of the whole manufacturing process and to ensure it continuous beats, it should be tuned with fine accuracy and high quality standard.

Slitting process is the origin where quality standard starts, and subsequently it is maintained by the different types of cutting process. Quality of the material is always on top priority at Sarjani we stress more on slitting process for better quality in every single process.
